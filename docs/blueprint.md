# **App Name**: ZELESTE ENGINEERS AND CONTRACTORS LLP

## Core Features:

- About Us Section: Detailed information about the organization's mission, history, and team.
- Programs & Services Page: Showcase the different programs and services offered by the organization.
- News & Updates Feed: Display latest news, announcements, and updates related to the organization.
- Contact Us Form: Allow visitors to send messages or inquiries to the organization.
- Contact Information Display: Provide clear and accessible contact information, including address, phone number, and email.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#30475E) to convey trust and professionalism. This color gives a sense of stability and authority, appropriate for an informational site.
- Background color: Light gray (#D3D3D3) to provide a neutral backdrop and ensure readability.
- Accent color: Soft gray-blue (#6A7F95) to complement the primary color while maintaining a clean aesthetic.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif font that offers both modernity and a touch of warmth, is recommended for readability.
- Headline font: 'PT Sans', to maintain consistency throughout the site. 'PT Sans' is a sans-serif that is legible and clean.
- Use simple, professional icons that align with the organization's brand and mission. Icons should be monochromatic in a shade of blue.
- Clean, grid-based layout with clear sections for easy navigation.
- Subtle animations for smooth transitions and enhanced user experience.
